Amazing Mart Performance Dashboard (EU)

Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales across segments, countries, categories, and yearly trends.

Author: Paul (SUT ZAW AUNG) Project: Amazing Mart BI Dashboard Date: 2025

Dashboard Overview

This interactive Power BI dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights. It provides a clear view of performance across customer segments, regions, categories, and year-to-year trends. These insights help improve marketing targeting, operational planning, and long-term strategy.

1. Sales by Segment

The donut chart shows how revenue is distributed across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments. Understanding segment performance helps identify the most valuable customer groups and shape marketing priorities.

Insight: Consumer segment generates the highest sales and shows strong buying behavior.

2. Sales by Country

The map visual compares performance across major European countries. Large bubbles represent high sales volumes, highlighting areas with strong customer demand and growth potential.

Insight: France, Germany, and the UK lead the region. Smaller markets offer opportunities for expansion.

3. Sales by Category

This chart reveals which product categories generate the most revenue. It helps businesses identify profitable items and evaluate which categories may need strategic adjustments or promotion.

Insight: Technology leads as the strongest category, showing high demand and profitability.
