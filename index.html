About Me

Hello! I am Sut Zaw Aung, a Digital Technology Innovation student at Kasem Bundit University (2024–present). I am deeply passionate about how data tells stories, uncovering patterns, driving decisions, and revealing insights that matter. My daily routine involves exploring datasets, experimenting with Python and R, and translating complex information into clear, actionable insights.
